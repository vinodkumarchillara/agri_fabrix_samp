About Agri Fabrix

With nearly three decades of experience in the agriculture sector and technology across India and Africa, our founding team has worked with leading institutions in areas such as agri-inputs, microfinance, and market linkages through Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs). Our journey includes promoting sustainable practices and providing agri-inputs and farm machinery solutions. We played an instrumental role in establishing one of India’s first FPOs in Koutla B, Telangana, which achieved a turnover of ₹8 crores by 2023, significantly increasing share value for its members. Despite these successes, many of India’s 14 crore farmers—predominantly small and marginal—face severe poverty, with average monthly incomes from farming at just ₹10,000. Although the number of farmer collectives has surpassed 100,000, many operate at low capacity and struggle to meet government income goals. Our initiative aims to address these challenges by leveraging technology to empower farmer collectives and progressive farmers for sustainable growth and prosperity
